Project Concept & Rules

Goal: Simulate an engaging continuous motion transitioning between 3 physical principles: Linear Motion -> Free Fall (Projectile Type 1) -> Circular Motion.

  • Continuous Physics: The final velocity from Linear Motion becomes the initial horizontal velocity for Free Fall. The final resultant velocity of Free Fall is transferred via a frictionless curved redirect into the Circular Loop.
  • Dual Panel Requirement: A smaller Secondary Panel is fixed at the bottom right of the simulation to trace the trajectory projection alongside the main animation.
  • Motion Analysis: Click "Show Motion Analysis" after running the simulation to view separated calculation results and tables for all 3 laws of motion.

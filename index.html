About Simulator

Emmre's Rollercoaster Simulator dynamically connects four continuous physical motions, creating a seamless track.

  • Linear Motion 1: Initial flat acceleration.
  • Free Fall (Projectile Type 1): Parabolic descent under gravity.
  • Curve Transition: Smooth circular blending into the next flat track.
  • Linear Motion 2: Flat track carrying inherited speed.
  • Circular Motion: A vertical loop testing centripetal logic.

Assets: Utilizes custom models including the Train[cite: 1] and kinematic formulas[cite: 2] for precise rendering.
